## üéØ Platform Overview

**MeritEdge** is a comprehensive, AI-powered assessment and evaluation platform designed to revolutionize how organizations identify, evaluate, and hire top talent. Built with cutting-edge technology and modern UX principles, MeritEdge transforms the traditional hiring process into a data-driven, efficient, and engaging experience.

### Mission Statement
*"Empowering organizations to make smarter hiring decisions through intelligent assessment technology and comprehensive candidate evaluation tools."*

### Vision
*"To become the global standard for talent assessment, helping companies build high-performing teams through data-driven hiring practices."*
